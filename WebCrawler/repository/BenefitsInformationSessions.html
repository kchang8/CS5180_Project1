Benefits Information Session

Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona

Benefits Information Sessions are provided to help guide employees through the process of choosing benefits through Cal Poly Pomona.

Information Sessions are conducted monthly to assist eligible employees in making an informed decision regarding their benefit choices. Following is the current information session schedule. For more information on employee eligibility see the Benefits Eligibility page. For further questions on benefit eligibility, please contact benefits@cpp.edu. Due to COVID-19 all information sessions will be held virtually. To register for the benefits information session, register on sumTotal.   Attendance at a Benefits Information Session is not mandatory to enroll in the benefits program; however, it is highly recommended.
