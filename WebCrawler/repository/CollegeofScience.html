Cal-Bridge Video

Cal-Bridge is a collaboration between 15 CSU and 9 UC campuses. The mission is to provide a bridge between the CSU and UC systems to increase the number of underrepresented students entering Ph.D. programs in physics or astronomy. The program is made possible by a National Science Foundation grant.
