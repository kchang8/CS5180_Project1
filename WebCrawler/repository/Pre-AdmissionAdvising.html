    Pre-Admission Advising

    Prospective Broncos from an international institution should contact the Office of Admissions for pre-admission advising. Our Outreach counselors assist prospective California-based students with pre-admission advising and can answer questions regarding the admissions process to Cal Poly Pomona, requirements, information about majors and programs, and details regarding important deadlines. Contact our office to schedule an appointment at 909-869-3529, or email  outreach@cpp.edu, or fill our contact request form. Walk-in pre-admission advising is available weekdays from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. permitting availability of counselors. Our office is located in the Student Services Building (121) on the first floor. 

    If you have coursework or hold a degree from an out-of-state or international institution, please contact the Office of Admissions; all other California-based students please contact our Outreach office.
