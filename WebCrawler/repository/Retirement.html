    Retirement

    The CSU provides eligible employees with a number of retirement benefits, including retirement income, medical and dental coverage, and voluntary retirement savings opportunities to help you build a financially secure future.

    Retirement program eligibility is based on appointment type, duration, full-time equivalency (FTE) and previous public agency or reciprocal agency employment. The state-mandated retirement program is the California Public Employees Retirement System (CalPERS) and is coordinated with Social Security (with the exception of those in industrial/police membership categories).  Employees not eligible for/excluded from CalPERS membership are covered by the CSU Part-time Seasonal Retirement Plan.
